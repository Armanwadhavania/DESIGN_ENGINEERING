Blood Donation Information

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 18-65 years
  • Weight: Minimum 50 kg
  • Good general health and feeling well on the day of donation
  • No recent infections, surgeries, or major illnesses
  • Free of diseases such as HIV, Hepatitis B, and C

How to Prepare

  • Eat a healthy meal before donating
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water
  • Avoid alcohol or heavy exercise before donation
  • Bring a valid ID

Benefits of Donating Blood

  • Helps save lives
  • Promotes heart health by reducing iron levels
  • Free mini health check-up
  • Boosts emotional well-being
